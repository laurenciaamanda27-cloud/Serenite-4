About Serenité

Founded in 2017, Serenité is privately owned and operated by CEO Anastasya Clarissa Kiem. With a keen eye, Anastasya was able to identify a missing piece in the women's apparel market by bringing sexy clubwear and jeans for women to the forefront of the affordable fashion industry. Anastasya says, "We've revolutionized fashion by making our customers part of the conversation from concept to delivery. We stay ahead of the fashion curve and the competition by engaging with our community daily to ensure we're delivering what they need as quickly as possible." She pioneered the use of Instagram as a platform to connect and engage with customers in a way never before possible. Through this first milestone, Serenite's success was born. Serenite opened its first location in Plaza Singapore.

"True luxury is finding serenity in every thread."
